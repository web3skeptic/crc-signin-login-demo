Log in with your Circles account

This is a standalone website. To sign you in, it embeds the Circles /crc-signin connector in an <iframe> and waits for it to report a connected address over postMessage. Your passkey and keys never leave the Circles iframe — this site only ever sees your address.

Sign in

Use the Circles connector on the right. Click Log in to authenticate with your device passkey, or Create account if you're new.

  • The connector must be served from a *.gnosis.io host for an existing passkey to be found (passkeys are bound to the gnosis.io domain).
  • This page grants the iframe allow="publickey-credentials-get *; publickey-credentials-create *", which WebAuthn requires for cross-origin passkey use.
